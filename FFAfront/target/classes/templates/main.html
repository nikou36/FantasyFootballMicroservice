Welcome to the archives of the our fantasy football league

Founded in 2017, the NFFL has hosted many fierce fantasy football battles. This site is dedicated to archiving the past seasons. Whether your here to reminice, or find proof for your smack talk you can find all the past history here.

2017 Season

At a Glance:

The first year of the National Frank Fantasy League ended with a final showdown between the 11-2 Cleveland Bellichicks(Nick) and the 9-4 Compton Cool Cats(Kellen). Kellen defeats Nick and becomes the first ever champion of the NFFL.

2018 Season

At a Glance:

The second year of the National Frank Fantasy League saw new faces and fierce competition culminating in a final showdown between James and the defending champion Kellen. Kellen decisively defeats James and takes his second title in two years. A dynasty is born.
